# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 2026**

## 1. Introduction

SYNTV Online ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

## 2. Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address
- Full name
- Profile avatar (optional)

### Usage Data
- Playlist URLs and credentials you add
- Viewing history and preferences
- Favorite channels, movies, and series
- App settings and configurations

### Device Information
- Device model and operating system version
- App version and crash reports
- Network status information

## 3. How We Use Your Information

- To provide and maintain our service
- To authenticate your identity and manage your account
- To process your IPTV playlists and deliver content
- To improve and optimize the application
- To send service-related notifications
- To detect, prevent, and address technical issues

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored securely on Supabase PostgreSQL databases
- Playlist credentials (Xtream passwords) are encrypted at rest
- We implement Row Level Security to ensure data isolation between users
- We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated data for analytics purposes.

## 6. Your Rights

- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your account and associated data
- Export your data
- Withdraw consent at any time

## 7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. After account deletion, we delete or anonymize your data within 30 days.

## 8. Third-Party Services

- **Supabase**: Database and authentication provider
- **Stripe**: Payment processing (if you subscribe)
- **Expo**: Application build and deployment

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.

## 10. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at: support@syntv.com
